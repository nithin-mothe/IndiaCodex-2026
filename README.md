## Project Description

TrustPay Protocol is a decentralized escrow platform built on the Cardano blockchain that enables secure buyer–seller transactions without relying on centralized intermediaries. Funds are securely locked in a smart contract until both parties agree that the transaction has been completed. If a dispute occurs, the platform provides an AI-assisted dispute resolution system to help users understand evidence and reach a fair outcome.

The platform combines blockchain transparency, decentralized escrow, secure authentication, and AI assistance to make online transactions safer, faster, and more trustworthy.

---

# Problem Statement

Online buyers and sellers frequently face fraud, payment disputes, fake products, and delayed fund settlements. Existing escrow services are mostly centralized, expensive, and require users to trust third parties.

TrustPay solves these problems by providing a decentralized escrow system where:

- Buyers' funds remain protected.
- Sellers receive guaranteed payment after successful delivery.
- Transactions are transparent and tamper-proof.
- AI assists users during disputes.

---

# Solution

TrustPay introduces a Cardano-native decentralized escrow protocol that securely manages transactions between buyers and sellers.

Workflow:

1. Buyer connects Cardano wallet.
2. Buyer creates an escrow transaction.
3. ADA is locked into escrow.
4. Seller completes the service or ships the product.
5. Buyer confirms delivery.
6. Funds are automatically released.
7. If disputes arise, AI assists both parties with evidence analysis and guidance.
